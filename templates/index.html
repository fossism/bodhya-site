Why Bodhya?

The tech ecosystem in India is growing rapidly. For too long, most opportunities flowed towards metros and IITs while overlooking the immense potential within Bihar. Students from tier-2 and tier-3 colleges, especially in Bihar, rarely get a fair shot. Talent is universal, but opportunity is not. until now, Bodhya exists to build the bridge.

Since January 2026, Bodhya has been changing that story by ending barriers and ensuring that the grit, talent, and ambition of Bihar don't just get a fair shot, but a center stage in the future of technology.
